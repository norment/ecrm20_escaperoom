KEYNOTE
SPEAKERS

ATHANASIA MOWINCKEL
Athanasia Mowinckel (Mo) is a neuroscientist working as staff scientist at the Center for Lifespan Changes in Brain and Cognition (LCBC) at the University of Oslo, developing methods for data collection, quality control and storage. Mo uses reproducible and open science methods in her work, including the development of open-source R packages such as ggseg. Mo is actively supportive of women in science and is a driving force behind the Oslo chapter of R-Ladies.
JAMES HEATHERS
James Heathers is a research scientist and self- described “data sleuth”. He is currently the Chief Scientific Offer at Cipher Skin in Boulder, Colorado. He is also co-host of the Everything Hertz podcast together with Dan Quintana, where they discuss everything to do with research and science methodology, academic life, and whatever makes the rounds on Science Twitter. He has long been an advocate for open and accessible psychological science.
